ChemConvert allows you to calculate common chemical and health equations using non-standard units.

Chemical equations that you can convert: Moles Ideal Gas equation Gibbs Free Energy Beer Lambert Law

Health equations that you can convert: Body Fat - Male Body Fat - Female BMI

Version 1.0 includes common units, more exotic units are on the way soon!
